## Tier Overview

**Tier 3** is built for organizations with demanding workloads. You unlock it once your cumulative top-ups reach **$50 USD equivalent**. At Tier 3 you get high rate limits across virtually every model in the catalogue — ideal for businesses integrating AI deeply into their core products, customer-facing features, and high-traffic services. The next step up is Tier 4 at $250 cumulative.
