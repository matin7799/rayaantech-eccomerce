This document outlines the AvalAI Privacy Policy for all our services, including the chat platform and developer APIs. At AvalAI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information.
 
## Information Collection
We may collect your personal information when you use our services. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, account information, and any other information you provide to us.

## Use of Information
We use your personal information to provide and improve our services. This may include communicating with you, responding to support requests, updating services, and providing information and offers related to our services.

Additionally, your information and conversations with the AI assistant will only be used to improve and address weaknesses in the language model if the "Help improve model" option in your account settings is enabled. This option is disabled by default. It should be noted that this information will not be shared with any third party, including business partners and service providers. You can manage the status of this option in your account settings, under the "Data" section.

In the process of processing your personal information, we will use interactive information generated with AI language models only for the purpose of improving and fixing model deficiencies in an anonymous manner, and your privacy will be maintained in this process. It should be noted that your identifying information will be removed in this process, and we will not use your information for any advertising or marketing purposes. This process is only performed if the "Help improve model" option is enabled.

We will not exploit any files uploaded by you. You are responsible for observing safety standards when uploading files and are prohibited from uploading sensitive and confidential files.

**API Privacy Protection: We do not process or exploit any user data from API calls made through our developer platform (API) and API endpoints. This includes no collection, storage, or use of any content from the API call body, such as prompts, inputs, messages, audio, or images. We only store API call history (such as model name and IP address) for pricing calculations and the safety and security of user APIs, not for collecting private user data!**

## Information Retention
We reserve the right to use information for the purpose of improving and developing the language model, while maintaining privacy, for up to 30 days after you delete the information (excluding uploaded files). Files uploaded by you are deleted immediately upon your request.

## Information Sharing
We share your personal information only when we have the right or permission to do so. This may include affiliates, business partners, and third-party service providers.

## Security
To protect your privacy at AvalAI, we use appropriate security technologies and management procedures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

All sensitive information, including exchanged conversations, is stored encrypted in the database.

Files uploaded by you are stored encrypted (SHA256).

## Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of your personal information.

You have the right to delete your account.

To exercise your rights, please contact us.

## Cookies
If you leave a comment on our site, you can choose to save your name, email address, and website in cookies. These cookies are for your convenience so that you do not have to enter your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies will last for one year.

If you visit our login page, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for five days, and screen options cookies last for one year. If you select "Remember Me," your login will persist for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

## Embedded Content from Other Sites
Articles and posts on this site may include embedded content (e.g., videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We commit to notifying you of any significant changes to the privacy policy.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions, suggestions, or criticisms about our privacy policy, please contact us.
